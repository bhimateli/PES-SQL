Top 10 Window Function Interview Questions

1. Running Total per Group

Show cumulative sales for each crop by date.

SUM(quantity_kg) OVER (PARTITION BY crop_id ORDER BY sale_date)

2. Rank Farmers by Total Sales

Rank farmers based on total sales.

RANK() OVER (ORDER BY total_sales DESC)

3. Top 3 Crops per Farmer

Get top 3 crops sold by each farmer.

ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY farmer_id ORDER BY total_sales DESC)

4. Remove Duplicate Records

Keep only latest sale per farmer and crop.

ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY farmer_id, crop_id ORDER BY sale_date DESC)

5. Compare Current vs Previous Sale

Show difference between current and previous sale.

LAG(quantity_kg) OVER (PARTITION BY crop_id ORDER BY sale_date)

6. First and Last Sale per Crop

Get first and last sale price.

FIRST_VALUE(price_per_kg) OVER (...) LAST_VALUE(price_per_kg) OVER (...)

7. Moving Average

Calculate 3-day moving average.

AVG(quantity_kg) OVER (ORDER BY sale_date ROWS BETWEEN 2 PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW)

8. Sales Above Average

Find sales greater than average.

AVG(quantity_kg) OVER (PARTITION BY crop_id)

9. Divide Data into Buckets

Split farmers into 4 groups.

NTILE(4) OVER (ORDER BY total_sales DESC)

10. Find Gaps in Dates

Detect missing sales dates.

LAG(sale_date) OVER (PARTITION BY crop_id ORDER BY sale_date)
